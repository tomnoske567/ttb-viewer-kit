# Drop your data here

Anything with your audience's real words in it:

- YouTube / podcast transcripts (`.txt`, `.md`, `.srt` — anything readable)
- Sales call or coaching call transcripts
- Screenshots of Instagram comments and DMs (images are fine — Claude reads them)
- Testimonials, reviews
- Emails people have sent you, survey answers
- Posts from your community (Skool, Circle, Discord, Facebook groups)

Messy is fine. Unsorted is fine. Claude does the digging.

**Nothing here leaves your computer.** This folder is raw material for your document, not something that gets uploaded anywhere.

Don't have anything yet? That's fine too — just say "build my viewer doc" and the interview will carry it.
