# Time to Build — Know Your Viewer Kit

Build the one document that makes everything in your business easier: a profile of exactly who your viewer is, written in their own words.

I have a 22-page version of this for my own business. It's why content ideas come easy, why my videos feel like a conversation, and why selling doesn't feel like selling. This kit walks you through building yours — with Claude doing the heavy lifting.

## What you get

- **A guided build.** Claude interviews you and digs through your real data — YouTube transcripts, call recordings, DMs, comments, testimonials — and pulls out who your viewer actually is.
- **A living document** (`MY-VIEWER.md`) you keep using with Claude forever. New comments and calls make it smarter over time.
- **A one-page dashboard** (`MY-VIEWER-DASHBOARD.html`) that presents it all beautifully. Double-click, opens in your browser.

No coding. No installs beyond Claude itself. You talk, it builds.

## How to use it

### Option 1 — Claude Code (best experience)

1. Click the green **Code** button at the top of this page → **Download ZIP** → unzip it. (Or clone the repo if you know git.)
2. Drop any data you have into the `my-data/` folder — transcripts, call recordings' transcripts, screenshots of DMs and comments, testimonials. Don't have any? Skip this. The interview covers you.
3. Open the folder in [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) and say:

   **"build my viewer doc"**

4. Answer the questions. One at a time, like a conversation. At the end you'll have your document and your dashboard.

### Option 2 — claude.ai (no downloads)

1. Download just one file from this repo: `.claude/skills/viewer-doc/SKILL.md`
2. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai), start a new chat, and attach that file along with any transcripts or screenshots you have.
3. Say: **"Follow this skill and build my viewer doc."**

You'll get the same document, delivered in the chat.

## What data helps (all optional)

- YouTube or podcast transcripts
- Sales call or coaching call recordings (transcribed)
- Instagram comments and DMs (screenshots work)
- Testimonials, reviews, emails from your audience
- Survey answers, community posts

The more real language from real people you feed it, the better your document gets. Zero data still works — the interview alone builds a solid first version, and the document marks what still needs validating.

## Keeping it alive

This is a living document, not a one-time exercise. Every time you get new comments, new calls, or a new customer, open the folder again and say **"update my viewer doc."** Six months from now it will know your audience better than you do.
